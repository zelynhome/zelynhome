Why Deep-Fit Engineering Matters

Many EU mattresses measure between 24 and 30 cm in depth. Once a topper is added, the combined stack commonly reaches 30 to 35 cm.

Standard 25 cm pocket sheets are underspecified for this configuration. They lose corner engagement under movement and after repeated wash cycles.

ZELYN is engineered around real-world mattress stacks, with depth allowance and perimeter retention designed for stable fit under daily use.

The Structure of Percale

Percale uses a balanced 1/1 weave architecture. This structure supports consistent airflow and thermal release through the fabric plane.

The surface finish is matte rather than glossy, with a crisp hand-feel that remains stable over time.

Compared with brushed cotton, percale has lower pilling risk and maintains a cleaner, more structured surface tension profile.
