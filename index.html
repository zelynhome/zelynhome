Our Story

ZELYN begins in the cotton fields of South India — where generations of growers cultivate high-quality 100% cotton with care and responsibility.

We partner with experienced textile mills to transform this cotton into breathable, long-lasting percale sheets crafted for European beds. Every sheet is designed to feel crisp, refined, and quietly luxurious.

From South India to Berlin and across Europe, ZELYN represents comfort with conscience.

Features & Benefits

  • 100% Cotton Percale — breathable and crisp.
  • Responsibly Sourced — from South Indian cotton growers.
  • Deep 35cm Fit — designed for European mattresses.
  • Durable Construction — built for long-term use.
  • Minimal Design — timeless and modern aesthetic.
  • All-Season Comfort — ideal for European climates.
