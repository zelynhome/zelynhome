Our Story

Our journey begins in the cotton fields of South India, where generations of growers cultivate cotton with care, tradition, and deep respect for their land.

We transform this cotton into soft, breathable organic percale — woven in responsible mills and crafted by skilled textile makers. The result is a fitted sheet that feels naturally crisp, quietly luxurious, and made for long-term comfort.

From South Indian mills to modern homes across Berlin and Europe, ZELYN exists to offer considered comfort with global standards and local integrity.
