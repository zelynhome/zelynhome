Why percale?

Percale is a plain weave that stays airy and cool. It feels crisp at first, then becomes softer while keeping a clean, neat look.

If you prefer “silky and drapey”, sateen is better. If you want “fresh and breathable”, percale wins.

Our Story

ZELYN began in Karur, South India — a textile region known for generations of weaving, stitching, and export-grade home linen production.

We grew up around real production: quality checks, consistent stitching, and cotton expertise that comes from years of doing it properly.

In Europe, we wanted bedding that felt premium but honest. Minimal, durable, calming — and most importantly: a fitted sheet that actually fits EU mattresses.

What we stand for

  • Fit first: made for real EU mattress dimensions
  • Material truth: breathable matte percale (not glossy)
  • Clean design: no loud branding, no clutter
  • Better choices: safer materials, sensible packaging

Starting simple

We start with one essential: the fitted sheet. Once fit and fabric are perfect, we expand into pillowcases and duvet covers.
