Why percale?

Percale is a plain weave that stays airy and cool. It feels crisp at first, then becomes softer while keeping a clean structure. If you sleep warm, percale is usually the best choice.

If you prefer “silky and heavy”, sateen is better. If you want “fresh and breathable”, percale wins.

Our Story

ZELYN began in the textile heartland of Karur, South India — a region known for generations of weaving, stitching, and export-grade home linen production.

We grew up around mills and workshops. Real production. Quality checks. Consistent stitching. People who understand cotton by feel — not by marketing terms.

In Europe, we wanted bedding that felt premium but honest. Minimal. Durable. Calm. A sheet that fits properly and lasts beyond seasons.

What we stand for

  • Fit first: made for real EU mattress dimensions
  • Material truth: breathable, matte percale (not glossy)
  • Simple design: no loud branding, no clutter
  • Better choices: safer materials, sensible packaging

What we won’t do

  • Overpromise softness on day one (percale starts crisp — that’s the point)
  • Use heavy “luxury” language to hide average quality
  • Sell bundles before the one essential is perfect

Indian roots. European living.
