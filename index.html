Our Story

ZELYN began in the textile heartland of Karur, South India — a region known for generations of weaving expertise, cotton craftsmanship, and honest textile production.

Growing up around mills, we saw the integrity and pride behind every thread: the rhythm of looms, the skill passed down through families, and the natural understanding of cotton that only time can teach.

When we moved to Europe, we noticed something missing — bedding that felt both premium and genuine. Something minimal, durable, and calming. Something that simply works.

ZELYN blends:

  • South Indian cotton expertise
  • European minimalist design
  • Organic, ethical sourcing
  • A commitment to honesty & comfort

Your fitted sheet is the result of both worlds:
Indian roots. European living.
