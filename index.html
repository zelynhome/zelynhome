Our Story

ZELYN began in the textile heartland of Karur, South India — a region known for generations of cotton weaving, handcrafted excellence, and ethical production.

Growing up around mills and weaving units, we saw firsthand the level of care that goes into every thread: the rhythmic clatter of looms, the expertise passed from parent to child, and the integrity of people who take pride in making something that lasts.

When we arrived in Europe, we noticed a gap — bedding that was often overpriced, overly processed, or lacking the authenticity we grew up with. ZELYN was created to bridge these two worlds:

  • South Indian craftsmanship
  • European minimal design
  • Ethically sourced organic cotton
  • Fair, transparent pricing

Our fitted sheet is the product of this philosophy — simple, honest, and thoughtfully made. A sheet that breathes, holds its shape, and ages gracefully with every wash.

This is more than bedding. It’s a connection between two homes:
Indian roots. European living.
