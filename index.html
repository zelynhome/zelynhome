Our Story

ZELYN began with a simple idea — essential bedding, done properly. Growing up around the mills of South India, we knew the craftsmanship, heritage, and care woven into every metre of cotton. Yet, most bedding in Europe felt over-priced or over-designed.

So we focused on one essential: the fitted sheet. A sheet that sits neatly, feels naturally cool, holds its shape, and lasts beyond seasons. No unnecessary extras. No inflated markups. Just honest quality from mills that have perfected cotton for generations.
